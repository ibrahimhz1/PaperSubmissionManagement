Submission Guidelines

Ensure your submission meets all requirements for consideration

Paper Format

All submissions must follow the Springer LNCS format. Papers should not exceed 12 pages including references and appendices.

  • Use the official Springer LNCS template
  • 12-page limit (including references)
  • PDF format only
  • Anonymous submission for review

Language

All papers must be written in English. Non-native speakers are encouraged to have their papers proofread before submission.

Originality

Submissions must represent original work that has not been published previously and is not under review elsewhere.

  • Original, unpublished research
  • No simultaneous submissions
  • Proper citation of related work
  • Plagiarism check will be conducted

Review Process

All submissions undergo a double-blind peer review process by at least three program committee members.
