About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind the Research Paper Management System

Our Story

Founded in 2018, the Research Paper Management System (RPMS) was born out of a need to streamline the academic publication process. Our founders, experienced researchers and academics themselves, recognized the challenges faced by scholars, reviewers, and publishers in managing the complex workflow of research paper submission, review, and publication.

What started as a small project to help a few university departments has grown into a comprehensive platform used by researchers, academic institutions, and publishers worldwide. Today, we serve over 50 universities and facilitate the publication of thousands of research papers annually.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence has made us a trusted partner in the academic community, helping to advance knowledge dissemination through efficient and reliable paper management solutions.

Our Mission

To revolutionize academic publishing by providing an intuitive, efficient, and transparent platform that simplifies the research paper management process for authors, reviewers, and publishers worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for research paper management, fostering collaboration and accelerating the dissemination of knowledge across all academic disciplines.
