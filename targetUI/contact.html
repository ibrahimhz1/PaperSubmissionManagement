Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform

How do I get started with RPMS?
Getting started is easy! Simply register for an account, verify your email, and you'll have access to our platform. You can then submit papers, manage reviews, or set up your institution's account depending on your role.
What types of institutions use RPMS?
Our platform is used by universities, research institutions, academic publishers, and scholarly societies worldwide. We serve organizations of all sizes, from small colleges to large research universities.
How secure is my research data?
We take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We employ industry-standard security measures and regularly undergo security audits to ensure your research remains protected.
Can I integrate RPMS with my existing systems?
Yes! We offer API access and can integrate with popular learning management systems, institutional repositories, and single sign-on providers. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific integration needs.
What support options are available?
We offer multiple support channels including email, phone, and live chat during business hours. We also provide comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and dedicated account managers for enterprise clients.
Do you offer training for new users?
Absolutely! We offer onboarding sessions, webinars, and customized training for institutions. We also have a resource library with guides and best practices to help users make the most of our platform.
